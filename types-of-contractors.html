Types of Contractors and How We Fit In

Different home projects call for different expertise. Choosing the right professional makes all the difference in ensuring safety and long-term quality.

Home Access Companies

These specialists focus on installations like ramps, stair lifts, wheelchair lifts, ceiling lifts, and residential elevators. We have decades of experience working closely with occupational therapists and clients to match the right equipment to each person's needs. At One Source Mobility, this is our foundation.

General Contractors

For large renovations like full bathroom remodels, accessible kitchens, or in-law suites (ADUs), we provide comprehensive project management. As a California B-General Building Contractor (License #1032291), we can design, manage, and execute every phase — from permitting to final walkthrough — or collaborate seamlessly with your preferred builder.

Handyman Services

Even small updates make a big impact. We handle grab bar installations, accessible door hardware, motion lighting, sturdy railings, and toilet upgrades for every level of mobility. Our attention to detail ensures these smaller modifications are installed safely and correctly.

Whenever possible, work with CAPS Certified (Certified Aging-in-Place Specialists) like One Source Mobility. We're trained to design spaces that evolve with your lifestyle — safe, functional, and beautiful for all ages and abilities.
