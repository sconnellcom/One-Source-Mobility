Essential VA Housing Grants

Access funding tailored for service-connected disabilities—up to lifetime maximums for ramps, lifts, bathroom remodels, and more.

HISA Grant

Up to $6,800 lifetime (service-connected) or $2,000 for others. Covers medically justified modifications like grab bars, widened doors, and ramps.

Home Improvements and Structural Alterations Grant for service-connected disabilities or 50%+ rating.

SAH Grant

Up to $121,812 (FY2026 est.) for severe disabilities (e.g., loss of limbs, blindness). Funds new builds, major adaptations, or mortgage reductions.

Specially Adapted Housing Grant for veterans with qualifying severe service-connected disabilities.

SHA Grant

Up to $25,350 (FY2026) for targeted changes like ramps or accessible kitchens. Ideal for existing homes.

Special Housing Adaptation Grant for veterans needing modifications to their current residence.

Temporary Residence Adaptation (TRA) adds up to $50,961 (SAH-eligible) or $9,100 (SHA) for temporary living situations while you transition to permanent housing.
